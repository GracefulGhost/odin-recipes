Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless skinless chicken thighs (can used chicken breast)
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 2 medium zucchini
  • 8 oz mushrooms
  • 1/2 medium onion
  • 1/2 cup cheddar cheese shredded
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 tbsp buffalo sauce

Buffalo Chicken Rice Bowls

Chicken thighs/breast, rice, mushrooms, zucchini, and onion cooked together and mixed with buffalo sauce and cheese.

One thing to note is that I can't try this recipe yet because I need 2 pans alongside a select few items.

All credit -> Josh Cortis

Instructions

  1. Cook to yield 3 cups of cooked rice. (1 Cup of Dry Rice)
  2. In a large bowl add the chicken, a bit of oil, chili powder, salt, and pepper, Toss to coat.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium high heat and add some oil. Cook the chicken thighs for 4-5 minutes on each side. Cut them into a large dice and return to the pan to develop color.
  4. Wash and cut your zucchini into a small dice and mushrooms into a large dice. Cut the onion into a small dice.
  5. Heat a large skillet over medium high heat. Add oil and saute the onions for a couple of minutes
  6. Add a bit more oil and add in the mushrooms, season with salt to help bring out the water. Allow them to cook and develop color.
  7. Add in the zuchinni and cook until soft
  8. Add in 3 cups of cooked rice and mix everything together.

Plating

  1. This recipe makes 5 servings Divide your ingredients evenly 5 ways.
