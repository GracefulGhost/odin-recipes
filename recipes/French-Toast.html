Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup of Milk.
  • 1 tsp of sugar
  • 1/4 tsp of cinnamon
  • pinch of salt
  • Few drops of Vanilla extract

French Toast

Quick meal that uses cheap & easy to find ingredients

When done correctly, you'll have 4 to 5 slices

All Credit -> YouTube Channel Casual Cooking

Instructions

  1. Whisk the eggs together and then add all of the ingredients together while mixing.
  2. Set pan to medium heat. 1/2 tablespoon of butter and 1 teaspoon of vegetable oil.
  3. let the ingredients get hot and take the bread and dip it each side into the egg mix.
  4. Put into the pan and wait a 1-2 minutes and keep adding a little of butter and oil with each slice.

Plating

  1. Syrup is optional but it's french toast. Just don't use so much of it.
